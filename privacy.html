Privacy Policy for Chrome extension

Effective date: May 2025 · Version 1.0

1. Data Collection – ZERO

PrivacySnapshot does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, URLs, or page content. The extension works entirely locally inside your browser using Chrome’s built-in APIs. We have no servers, no analytics trackers, and no remote logging.

2. Permissions usage (simple explanation)

activeTab – needed to access the current webpage when you click the extension icon.
scripting – reads DOM elements and network data to detect trackers and fingerprinting attempts.
webRequest – inspects outgoing third‑party requests and identifies tracking domains. All analysis stops when you close the popup.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

The extension reads cookie information (names, domains) solely to show you which cookies are tracking-related. It never modifies, deletes, or transmits your cookies. No cookie data leaves your device.

4. Third‑party services

We don’t integrate any external analytics, CDNs that log your IP, or support services that could track you. The only external requests are for loading fonts and icons (Google Fonts, FontAwesome) – but those are standard web assets. You may block them with browser settings.

5. Data retention & user rights

Since we collect nothing, there is no data to retain or delete. You have full control because everything stays inside your own browser. You can uninstall the extension at any time, and no residual data remains.

6. Contact for privacy concerns

If you have any questions, email privacy@privacysnapshot.app. We respond within 72 hours.

This policy is written to be honest and suitable for Chrome Web Store review. No legalese tricks.
