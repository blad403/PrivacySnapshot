Why we built this – no BS

Over the last few years, I got tired of seeing popups that say “we value your privacy” while 50+ trackers load behind the scenes. Most people have no idea that every click, every hover, and even your battery level can be used to fingerprint you.

PrivacySnapshot is my honest attempt to give you a tool that actually shows the truth – locally, without sending a single byte to any server. It’s not about fear; it’s about awareness. When you know what’s happening, you can decide whether to accept it or fight back.

No cloud, no monthly fees, no data selling. Just a simple extension that works.

You asked, we answer

Does it collect my browsing data?

Never. The extension works 100% offline. No data leaves your computer – not even the URL you're scanning.

Is scanning really local?

Yes. Every analysis uses Chrome's local APIs (webRequest, DOM inspection). No external servers involved.

Is it free forever?

Core features are completely free. No paywalls for privacy essentials. If we add advanced cloud features later, local version remains free.
